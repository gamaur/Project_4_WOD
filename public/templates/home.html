Welcome to Workout of the Day

Workout of the Day is an app for anyone wanting to quickly find a workout to spice up your gym routine. Find fun workouts based on the equipment you want to use by using the filter. If you have any great workouts to share with others feel free to sign-up and add it to the list. You can also leave comments on other workouts on this site. We hope we can help you pick the right workouts to make you break a sweat!
